About Us

Umwelt-Campus Birkenfeld, Hochschule Trier

Umwelt-Campus Birkenfeld is a unique institution of Hochschule Trier, located in a beautiful natural environment. It focuses on sustainability, environmental studies, and practical applications of technology to create a better future.

About the LCA Demonstrator

The LCA Demonstrator is an educational tool designed to provide insight into Life-Cycle Assessment (LCA) principles. It aims to simplify complex topics and enhance learning through a structured, interactive experience.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to promote sustainability and environmental awareness through technology, education, and practical solutions. We strive to make LCA concepts accessible to all through innovative tools and resources.
